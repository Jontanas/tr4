TR4

Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

The short version: TR4 does not collect, store or share any personal data. Your recordings live on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads and no tracking of any kind.

Your recordings

Audio you record with TR4 is stored only on your iPhone, inside the app's own documents folder. It is never uploaded to us — we have no servers. Recordings leave your device only when you export or share them using the iOS share sheet, to a destination you choose.

Microphone access

TR4 asks for microphone permission so you can record. Audio from the microphone (or from a connected USB audio device) is written directly to your recordings and used for nothing else. You can revoke this permission at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone.

Transcription

The optional transcription feature uses Apple's Speech Recognition. When possible, recognition runs entirely on your device. For some languages, iOS may send the audio to Apple's servers for processing, subject to Apple's privacy policy. TR4 only receives the resulting text, which is saved locally with your project. Transcription only runs when you open the transcription screen and start an analysis.

What we don't do

  • No analytics or telemetry SDKs
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No accounts, logins or profiles
  • No third-party data sharing — there is no data to share
  • No tracking across apps or websites

Children

TR4 does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future feature requires a service — the updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date before the feature ships.
